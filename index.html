Poketh

Blockchain Collectives

We created a new way to create and distribute your favorite blockchain assets.

Blockchain collectibles are unique items that are created, owned, and traded on-chain. They are owned by you, directly.

Poketh's implementation of collectibles mimics the traditional sense of thrill when opening a pack of trading cards. As time goes by, users might find some very rare collectibles that other players might want to buy for thousands of dollars. Since the items are blockchain based, they cannot be counterfeit!

ERC891

What is ERC891?

ERC891 is an innovative way of distributing virtual assets based on blockchain.

Dapps currently use distribution methods that fall under the categories of ICO or airdrop. Both have problems and are quickly becoming obsolete.

ERC891 uses a novel distribution method that is similar to mining. It creates a more organic growth of the Dapp with fairer distribution.
